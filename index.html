GROW UP

Straws were made for kids to help them get liquid in their mouth without making mess. You are a fucking adult, act like one. First put your Coke or carmel machhiato down and get a real drink, coffee (black), beer, liquor, or water. Next put your lips up to the side of your cup and tilt. And shut the fuck up about getting soft drinks from fast food places or convenient stores. Get a real meal. And tell them to keep the straw.
